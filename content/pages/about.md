## What This Site Is

DermaProtocol is a decision-support tool. It helps you understand your options, compare ingredients, build simple routines, and avoid common mistakes &mdash; all in one place.

Every page is structured around practical action: what works, what is safe, what fits your goal, and what to skip.

## What This Site Is Not

DermaProtocol is not a medical resource, a diagnostic tool, or a replacement for professional advice. It does not claim to treat, cure, or prevent any condition.

We do not sell products directly. Where product recommendations appear in the future, they will be clearly labelled and structured around usefulness, not sales pressure.

## How Recommendations Are Framed

We use conservative, evidence-aware language throughout. You will see phrases like "may help," "evidence is stronger here," and "results vary" rather than miracle claims or guaranteed outcomes.

Evidence strength is labelled on every ingredient page: **strong**, **moderate**, **limited**, or **emerging**. This helps you make informed decisions rather than relying on hype.

## Why Simplicity Matters

Most people do not need a 12-step routine. Overcomplication leads to irritation, wasted money, and poor adherence. DermaProtocol favours minimal, effective protocols over maximalist routines.

Start simple. Add slowly. Stop when something is not working.
