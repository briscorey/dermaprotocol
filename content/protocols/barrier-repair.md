<div class="detail-section">
<h2 class="detail-section__title">Who This Is For</h2>
<div class="detail-section__body">
<p>This protocol is for people experiencing signs of a damaged skin barrier: persistent dryness, tightness, stinging with products that did not previously sting, redness, and increased sensitivity. Common causes include over-exfoliation, too many actives, harsh products, and environmental stress.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="detail-section">
<h2 class="detail-section__title">Core Protocol</h2>
<div class="detail-section__body">

<div class="alert alert--tip" style="margin-bottom:var(--sp-6)">
<div class="alert__title">The key principle</div>
Stop all actives. Simplify radically. Let your skin recover before reintroducing anything.
</div>

<div class="routine-block">
<div class="routine-block__header">&#9728;&#65039; AM Routine</div>
<div class="routine-block__body">
<div class="routine-step"><div class="routine-step__order">1</div><div><div class="routine-step__name">Water rinse or very gentle cleanser</div><div class="routine-step__detail">No foaming, no fragrance</div></div></div>
<div class="routine-step"><div class="routine-step__order">2</div><div><div class="routine-step__name">Ceramide-rich moisturiser</div><div class="routine-step__detail">Look for ceramides, cholesterol, fatty acids</div></div></div>
<div class="routine-step"><div class="routine-step__order">3</div><div><div class="routine-step__name">Sunscreen SPF 30+</div><div class="routine-step__detail">Mineral/physical if skin is very reactive</div></div></div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="routine-block" style="margin-top:var(--sp-4)">
<div class="routine-block__header">&#127769; PM Routine</div>
<div class="routine-block__body">
<div class="routine-step"><div class="routine-step__order">1</div><div><div class="routine-step__name">Gentle cleanser</div><div class="routine-step__detail">Cream or oil-based</div></div></div>
<div class="routine-step"><div class="routine-step__order">2</div><div><div class="routine-step__name">Ceramide-rich moisturiser</div><div class="routine-step__detail">Apply to slightly damp skin</div></div></div>
<div class="routine-step"><div class="routine-step__order">3</div><div><div class="routine-step__name">Occlusive layer (optional)</div><div class="routine-step__detail">Petrolatum or squalane to seal in moisture overnight</div></div></div>
</div>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<div class="detail-section">
<h2 class="detail-section__title">When to Reintroduce Actives</h2>
<div class="detail-section__body">
<p>Wait until your skin no longer stings with basic products, feels comfortably hydrated, and redness has visibly calmed. This typically takes 2-6 weeks depending on the severity of the damage.</p>
<p>When reintroducing, add <strong>one product at a time</strong>, starting with the gentlest (niacinamide is a good first reintroduction). Wait 1-2 weeks between each new addition.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="detail-section">
<h2 class="detail-section__title">Common Mistakes</h2>
<div class="detail-section__body">
<ul>
<li>Reintroducing actives too soon because skin "feels better"</li>
<li>Using toner, exfoliants, or acids during the repair phase</li>
<li>Choosing moisturisers with fragrance or essential oils</li>
<li>Not giving the process enough time</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
